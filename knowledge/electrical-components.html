These components are a few of the many building blocks of analog circuits.

ComponentDescriptionSchematic Symbol
ResistorLimits current
CapacitorStores electric charge
InductorStores energy in a magnetic field
DiodeAllows current flow in one direction
TransistorAmplifies or switches electric signals
